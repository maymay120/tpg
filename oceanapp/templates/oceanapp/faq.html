Note: We've provided this section to answer general questions about the platform and how it functions, additionally to inform our users about what's going on in the industry. For any other questions please contact our dedicated support team.

FAQ

What is the minimum deposit or withdrawl amount?
The minimum withdrawal amount is €50. You can withdraw your profits at the end of each investment period, or request an early withdrawal. (See below.)
How long does it take to process withdrawls and deposits?
At the end of the investment period or if you want to withdraw your funds prior, we allow you to do this, simply go do your account page in the dashboard and request early withdrawal. Our dedicated IR personal will attend to you and initiate the transaction, you will however, need to verify the withdrawal.
Can i open more than one account?
Unfortunatley at this point and time, you can only open one account. We are working very hard to offer multiple accounts, and more features for you, our trusty investors.
Can i change my email?
Of Course! You may want to change your account's email for various reasons, you are free to do so in your settings page, in the dashboard. We will, however prompt you to verify your email address, otherwise, you won't be able to login until doing so.
How do i close my account?
To close your account, request early withdrawal from the dashboard, and specify to our IR staff during the process. Your account will be closed within 3 working days. You also have an option to cancel said request.

Take Note!

How to top up the balance?
After completing the registration process and personal identification and choosing your portfolio, you can top up your Ocean account with more Bitcoin. The minimum top-up amount is €250. Each account is only allowed to hold a maximum €10,000 in principle investment.
How to invest?
Sign up, verify your identity and deposit money into your account. After you top up the account, choose an investment strategy according to your income and risk expectations, create an investment portfolio under the required parameters. There you go — you have become our investor!
Who can become an investor?
We are also ready to welcome any, and all investors, but this requires an additional personal AML check. Each case is considered individually. If you are interested in Ocean or have more questions about us, please, send your request at support@oceanfortunes.com
How is an investment interest calculated?
The interest is accrued on a weekly basis. The income on your investment portfolio is deposited to your Ocean account every week, and the investment amount is automatically reinvested in new portfolios before the investment period expires. You can independently invest the income in new portfolios or request early withdrawal of funds if need be, however this isn't advised as it come with a holding charge.
Why do you need verification?
Ocean strongly follows the strict standards of the European anti-money laundering (AML) legislation, user verification, personal data storage and usage. On the one hand, the purpose of identity verification is to ensure and guarantee the security of your funds and transactions. On the other hand, it is meant to simplify business processes and to make them transparent and efficient. We ensure your secure identification via our proprietary system, in cooperation with our compliance department.

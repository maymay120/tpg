Frequently Ask Questions

What is Ocean Fortunes?

Ocean Fortunes is not a market place, it is a platform for investing, issued by the companies joint under Fintech Finance. Ocean Fortunes is included in ITSF holding. The platform offers a flexible investment concept that suits even beginning investors.

How to top up balance?

After completing the registration process, there are two ways you can top up your Ocean Fortunes account: using your personal debit/credit card or via our crypto payment gateway from your wallet. The minimum top-up amount is €10.

How to invest?

Sign up, verify your identity and deposit money into your Ocean Fortunes account. After you top up the account, choose an investment strategy according to your income and risk expectations, create an investment portfolio under the required parameters. Here you go — you have become our investor!

Who can become an investor?

Any individual or legal person with a crypto wallet can become an Ocean Fortunes investor. Each case will be considered individually. If you are interested in Ocean Fortunes or have more questions about us, please, send your request at support@oceanfortunes.com

How is an investment interest calculated?

The interest is accrued on a daily basis. The income on your investment portfolio is deposited to your Ocean Fortunes account every week, and the investment amount is automatically reinvested before the portfolio expires. You can independently invest the income in new portfolios or transfer it to your wallet.

How to withdraw funds?

At the end of the investment period or if you want to withdraw your funds prior, we allow you to do this, using your linked wallet or a credit/debit card, i.e. only that account from which the initial investment was deposited. If for any reason you need to withdraw your money to a different wallet from the original one, you will have to verify them first. The minimum withdrawal amount is €50.
